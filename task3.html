Smart Watch


A smartwatch is a portable and wearable computer device in a form of a watch;smartwatches provide a local touchscreen interface for daily use, while an associated
smartphone app provides management and telemetry, such long-term biomonitoring. While early models could perform basic tasks such as calculations, digital time
telling, translations, and game-playing, smartwatches released since 2015 have more general functionality closer to smartphones, including mobile apps,
a mobile operating system, and WiFi/Bluetooth connectivity. Some smartwatches function as portable media players, with FM
radio and playback of digital audio and video files via a Bluetooth headset.
